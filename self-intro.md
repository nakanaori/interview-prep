# Tell Me About Yourself

> Reference: [Tech Interview Handbook — Self Introduction](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/self-introduction/)

## Structure

1. **Start with basic background information**
   - Name
   - Past companies
   - Noteworthy project (best if it's a public consumer product they've heard of)

2. **KISS — Keep It Simple and Sweet**
   - Highlight projects, including numbers if impressive or challenges overcome
   - Formula: *"I do X by Y to Z."*

3. **Why do they want you?**
   - Is your experience relevant?
   - Similar tech stack or built relevant products?

4. **Practice!**

---

## Example Script

> Hi, I'm Varianto. I'm currently a Backend and Platform Engineer at GovTech Procurement Indonesia with around 4 years of experience in software engineering. Previously, I worked at SIRCLO, an e-commerce enabler in Indonesia, and at Bank Central Asia, one of the largest private banks in Indonesia.
>
> I mainly work with Go, building backend applications and services. I've worked extensively with GCP for cloud-native solutions and Temporal for workflow orchestration. I also have prior experience with JavaScript and Java.
>
> At GovTech Procurement, I designed and built the order and cart services from scratch. These systems now support over a million government procurement transactions per year. I also led a database consolidation project where we migrated from around 600 on-premise database instances into a single multi-tenant setup. That reduced database costs by over 99% while improving manageability and scalability.
